About Us

Our Story

Joy Madu started Sekanni in 2024 with a vision to combine traditional African bead artistry with modern design sensibilities. Each piece reflects a deep respect for African heritage and a passion for timeless elegance.

At Sekanniii's Empire, we prioritize authenticity and craftsmanship. Our beads are handcrafted by skilled artisans who use traditional techniques passed down through generations. From vibrant necklaces to intricate bracelets, each design is a masterpiece.
